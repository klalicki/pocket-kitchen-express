Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies are soft and chewy on the inside, with a light crust on the outside.
Time: about 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine

  • 1/2 cup white sugar

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 egg

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1/4 tsp salt

  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

350°

Preheat oven to 350° F.

Melt 1/2 cup butter or margarine in a microwave-safe bowl.

Add 1/2 cup white sugar and 1/4 cup brown sugar, and stir until smooth.

Add 1 tsp. vanilla and 1 egg, and stir until smooth.

Add 1/2 cup of flour, 1/2 tsp. baking soda, and 1/4 tsp. salt, and stir until smooth.

Add the remaining 1 cup of flour, and mix until fully combined.

Add 3/4 cup chocolate chips, and mix in with your hands.

Roll the dough into golf-ball-sized pieces, and place 2-3 inches apart on a baking sheet.

9:00

Bake in a 350° F oven for 9-11 minutes

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5-10 minutes before removing. To keep the cookies soft for longer, store them in an air-tight container.
